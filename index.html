Who We Are

Precision Data That Drives Decisions

Airowtek Private Limited is a Bangalore-based geospatial technology company specializing in 2D/3D data processing, photogrammetry workflows, LiDAR classification, CAD/GIS mapping, and BIM integration. We bridge the gap between raw spatial data and actionable digital intelligence.

Bringing extensive domain expertise and a team of certified geospatial engineers, we deliver survey-grade accuracy at scale — from single-site models to large infrastructure corridor mapping.

Photogrammetry Processing

High-accuracy 3D mapping and ORI generation from raw imagery

Lidar Classification

High-density point clouds with accurate ground and detailed classification.

GIS Analytics

Spatial analysis, change detection, and custom GIS dashboards.

BIM Integration

As-built BIM models conforming to LOD 300–400 standards.
