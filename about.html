Our Story

From Startup to
Industry Benchmark

Airowtek Private Limited was founded with a singular vision: to democratize access to precision geospatial data across India. Based in JP Nagar, Bangalore — India's technology heartland — we serve clients across infrastructure, mining, agriculture, smart cities, and more.

Our team of photogrammetrists, GIS specialists, and BIM engineers works with industry-leading processing software to deliver 2D and 3D outputs that meet international survey standards including ASPRS, ISO 19111, and BIM Level 2.
